About PackIt

PackIt is a peer‑to‑peer platform that connects people who want to send items with verified travelers who can carry them on their existing trips.

What we do

Senders post what they need to move (route, dates, weight). Travelers post their trip (route and capacity). PackIt helps both sides find a match, agree on a reward, and complete the hand‑off safely.

Why it matters

  • Speed: travelers are already moving between cities/countries.
  • Efficiency: makes use of unused luggage capacity.
  • Opportunity: travelers can earn extra money while they travel.
