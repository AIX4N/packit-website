How it works

For Senders

1) Create a shipment with route, dates, and item details.
2) Browse matching trips and propose a match.
3) Agree on a reward, meet up, and track the delivery.

For Travelers

1) Post your trip route and capacity.
2) Receive proposals from senders and accept the best one.
3) Carry the item and get rewarded after successful delivery.

PackIt is designed for everyday items and personal shipments. Always follow local laws and airline rules.
