Account & login

I didn’t receive the verification email

  • Check spam/junk folders.
  • Make sure you used the correct email address.
  • Try requesting a new verification email from the app (if available).

I forgot my password

  • Use “Forgot password?” on the login screen to request a reset link.
  • If the link expired, request a new one.

I can’t log in

  • Confirm your email is verified.
  • Check for typos in email/password.
  • If you still can’t access your account, email support@itspackit.com.

Approval / verification status

To keep PackIt safe, new accounts may require verification.

Pending approval: your documents are being reviewed. You’ll be notified once a decision is made.
Rejected: if we couldn’t verify your information, you may see a reason in the app. If you believe it’s a mistake, contact support.

Trips, shipments, and matches

Posting a trip

  • Fill in route, date/time, and available weight clearly.

Posting a shipment

  • Add accurate details, declared value, and an optional photo.

Match issues

  • If a match looks wrong or suspicious, contact support with a screenshot and the match ID (if visible).

Safety & prohibited items

PackIt is not a shipping company. Users must follow airline, customs, and local laws.

Never send or carry prohibited items, including (non-exhaustive):
  • Illegal substances, weapons, explosives
  • Hazardous chemicals
  • Counterfeit goods
  • Cash or high-risk valuables
  • Anything prohibited by the origin or destination laws
If you think a listing involves prohibited items, report it to support.

Reporting abuse or suspicious activity

If you suspect fraud, identity misuse, or unsafe behavior:

  1. Email support@itspackit.com
  2. Include:
    • Your account email
    • The user’s name/identifier (if visible)
    • Screenshots
    • A short description of what happened

We may suspend accounts that violate our rules or create risk for other users.

Data & privacy requests (GDPR)

We take privacy seriously and aim to follow GDPR principles (lawfulness, fairness, transparency, data minimization, and security).

You can contact support@itspackit.com to request:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of your profile details
  • Deletion of your account and personal data (where legally permitted)
  • Questions about how your data is stored and used

Account deletion

You can delete your PackIt account directly from within the app settings.

  • Your account is disabled immediately
  • Your information is retained for 30 days in case restoration is requested
  • After 30 days, personal data is permanently deleted or anonymized where legally required retention applies

If you experience any issues with account deletion, contact support.

Response times

We aim to respond within 1–5 business days. During busy periods, it may take a bit longer.
