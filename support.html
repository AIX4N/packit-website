Account & login

I didn’t receive the verification email
  • Check spam/junk folders.
  • Make sure you used the correct email address.
  • Try requesting a new verification email from the app (if available).
I forgot my password
  • Use “Forgot password?” on the login screen to request a reset link.
  • If the link expired, request a new one.
I can’t log in
  • Confirm your email is verified.
  • Check for typos in email/password.
  • If it still fails, email support@itspackit.com.

Trips, shipments & matches

Posting a trip
  • Fill in route, date/time, and available weight clearly.
  • Add notes if pickup/drop-off requires context.
Posting a shipment
  • Add accurate details, declared value, and an optional photo.
  • Choose realistic dates.
Match issues
  • If a match looks wrong or suspicious, contact support with a screenshot.
  • Include the match ID if visible.

Safety & prohibited items

PackIt is not a shipping company. Users must follow airline, customs, and local laws.

Never send or carry prohibited items, including (non-exhaustive): illegal substances, weapons, explosives, hazardous chemicals, counterfeit goods, cash, or anything illegal at origin/destination.

If you think a listing involves prohibited items, report it to support@itspackit.com.

Report abuse

If you suspect fraud, identity misuse, or unsafe behavior:

  • Email support@itspackit.com
  • Include your account email
  • Include the user’s name/identifier (if visible)
  • Add screenshots + a short description

We may suspend accounts that violate our rules or create risk for other users.

Data & privacy

You can contact support to request access to your data, correction of profile details, deletion (where legally permitted), or questions about how your data is used.

See our full policy here: Privacy Policy.

Account deletion

You can delete your PackIt account directly from within the app settings.

  • Your account is disabled immediately.
  • Your information is retained for 30 days in case restoration is requested.
  • After 30 days, personal data is permanently deleted or anonymized where legally required retention applies.

If you experience any issues, email support@itspackit.com.
